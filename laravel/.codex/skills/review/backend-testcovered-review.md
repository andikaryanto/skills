## Testing Checklist

Verify that:

- Do not run test.
- Every changed concrete application class is mapped to a dedicated test or an
  allowed documented exclusion.
- Endpoint coverage is not treated as a replacement for focused controller or
  middleware tests.
- New functionality is covered by tests.
- Happy paths are tested.
- Failure paths are tested.
- Business rules are tested.
- Complex queries are tested when applicable.
